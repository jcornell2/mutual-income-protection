8-Section Pre-Application Overview

Our intake wizard mirrors the information carriers require for disability income insurance applications.

Step 1

Personal Information

Legal name, date of birth, contact details, preferred contact method, residential address, gender, marital status, and dependents.

Step 2

Occupation & Duties

Provider type, specialty, career stage, employer, duties performed, hours worked, income sources, and licensure.

Step 3

Financial Profile

Annual income, expense ranges, assets, debts, student loans, home and vehicle values, and desired monthly benefit.

Step 4

Insurance History

Existing disability and life policies, health insurance status, prior denials, group coverage, and beneficiary details.

Step 5

Medical & Health

Height, weight, tobacco and substance use, medical conditions, medications, surgeries, and family history.

Step 6

Lifestyle & Risk

Exercise habits, hobbies, travel frequency, dangerous activities, DUI history, and military status.

Step 7

Disability Details

Current disability status, reason for applying, symptoms, work impact, referral source, and additional notes.

Step 8

Disclosures & Consent

Privacy consent, medical exam acknowledgment, agent follow-up acknowledgment, and premium targeting confirmation.
